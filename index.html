About us...

The idea of Gifted comes from a long time ago, when its creators were wrapping papers around well-thought gifts and told themselves that nothing was as satisfying as seeing the smile of a loved one when he or she opened the present. Juliette then decided everyone deserved to have the chance to see this - and created this start-up for those who love but don't have enough time or ideas to create the perfect gift.
